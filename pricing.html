Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SafetyCheck Pro™

How quickly will I receive inspection alerts?
Inspection alerts are typically sent within 15-30 minutes of the data being posted to the FMCSA database. We monitor the FMCSA system continuously throughout the day to ensure you receive timely notifications.
Do I need to provide my FMCSA Portal credentials?
No! SafetyCheck Pro™ uses publicly available FMCSA data. You never need to share your portal login credentials. Simply provide your DOT number, and we'll start monitoring immediately.
What happens when my fleet grows beyond 50 drivers?
When your fleet exceeds 50 drivers, you'll receive a notification to upgrade to our Professional plan. We offer a smooth transition with no interruption in service and a 14-day grace period to complete the upgrade.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! There are no contracts or cancellation fees. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time directly from your account settings. For paid plans, you'll have access until the end of your billing period.
What types of inspections are monitored?
We monitor all DOT inspection levels: Level 1 (Full), Level 2 (Walk-Around), Level 3 (Driver-Only), Level 4 (Special), Level 5 (Terminal), and Level 6 (Radioactive). You'll receive alerts for any violations, out-of-service orders, and clean inspections based on your preferences.
