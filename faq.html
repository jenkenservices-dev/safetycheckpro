Getting Started

What is SafetyCheck Pro? +
SafetyCheck Pro is a simple DOT inspection monitoring service. We check FMCSA data daily and notify you when new inspections are posted for your DOT number. It's that simple - no complex features, just the alerts you need.
Do I need to provide my FMCSA login credentials? +
No! We never ask for your FMCSA portal login. We only use publicly available data to monitor your fleet. Your credentials stay private.
How quickly can I get started? +
Setup takes about 5 minutes. Just sign up, enter your DOT number, and choose how you want to receive notifications. That's it - you'll start getting alerts as soon as new inspections post to FMCSA.

Pricing & Plans

Is it really free for small fleets? +
Yes! Fleets with 50 or fewer drivers can use SafetyCheck Pro for free. No hidden fees, no credit card required. This includes daily monitoring and notifications.
What if my fleet grows beyond 50 drivers? +
When you exceed 50 drivers, we'll notify you about upgrading to our Professional plan at $39/month for up to 200 drivers. You can monitor up to 3 DOT numbers on the Professional plan.
Are there any contracts? +
No contracts, no setup fees. Paid plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime.

How It Works

What information do you monitor? +
We monitor all inspection levels (1-6) posted to FMCSA. You'll see:
  • Inspection counts and dates
  • Location of inspections
  • Out-of-service status
  • Number of violations (not specific codes)
How often do you check for new inspections? +
We check FMCSA data daily. Most inspections appear in the FMCSA system within 24-48 hours of occurring, though some may take longer. We'll notify you as soon as we detect them.
How do I receive notifications? +
You can choose to receive notifications by text message or email. Free accounts get one notification recipient, while Professional accounts can add unlimited recipients.

Technical Questions

What browsers are supported? +
SafetyCheck Pro works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Our dashboard is mobile-friendly and works on phones and tablets too.
Is my data secure? +
Yes, we use SSL encryption for all data transmission. We only access public FMCSA data and never store sensitive information like driver details or violation specifics.
What if FMCSA data is delayed? +
FMCSA data entry can sometimes be delayed. We check daily and notify you as soon as inspections appear, but we can't control when officers enter the data initially.
