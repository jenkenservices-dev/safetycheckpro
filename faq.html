Getting Started

What is SafetyCheck Pro™? +
SafetyCheck Pro™ is an automated DOT compliance monitoring platform that tracks your fleet's roadside inspections and violations in real-time. We monitor public FMCSA data daily and send you instant alerts when new inspections or violations are recorded, helping you stay on top of compliance without constantly checking the FMCSA portal.
Do I need to provide my FMCSA login credentials? +
No! SafetyCheck Pro™ never requires your FMCSA portal login credentials. We access only publicly available data from FMCSA databases to monitor your fleet. Your login credentials remain completely secure and private.
How quickly can I get started? +
You can be up and running in less than 5 minutes! Simply:
  • Sign up with your basic information
  • Enter your DOT number
  • Configure your alert preferences
  • Start receiving real-time violation alerts
No complex setup, no contracts, and no waiting periods.

Pricing & Plans

Is SafetyCheck Pro™ really free for small fleets? +
Yes! Fleets with 50 or fewer drivers get full access to SafetyCheck Pro™ absolutely FREE. No hidden fees, no credit card required, no time limits. This includes all monitoring features, alerts, and reports.
What if my fleet grows beyond 50 drivers? +
When your fleet grows beyond 50 drivers, you'll be upgraded to our paid plan. We'll notify you in advance and provide flexible options. Our paid plans are competitively priced with no long-term contracts required. You can always scale up or down based on your fleet size.
Are there any setup fees or contracts? +
No setup fees and no contracts! SafetyCheck Pro™ operates on a month-to-month basis for paid plans. You can cancel anytime without penalties. We believe in earning your business through value, not locking you into contracts.

Features

What types of violations do you monitor? +
We monitor all DOT violations recorded in FMCSA databases, including:
  • Hours of Service violations
  • Vehicle maintenance issues
  • Driver qualification violations
  • Hazmat violations
  • Drug and alcohol violations
  • All roadside inspection levels (I-VI)
You can customize which violations trigger immediate alerts.
Can I track my CSA scores? +
Yes! SafetyCheck Pro™ provides comprehensive CSA BASIC score tracking across all seven categories. You can view current scores, track trends over time, and receive alerts when scores change. Our dashboard makes it easy to identify areas needing improvement.
Do you provide compliance reports? +
Yes, we offer comprehensive compliance reports that include violation summaries, CSA score analysis, inspection history, and trend analysis. Reports can be generated on-demand or scheduled for regular delivery. Perfect for safety meetings, insurance renewals, or regulatory audits.

Technical Questions

How often do you check for new violations? +
We monitor FMCSA databases continuously throughout the day. Our system checks for new inspections and violations every 15-30 minutes during business hours. As soon as a new violation is detected for your fleet, you'll receive an alert based on your notification preferences.
Is my fleet data secure? +
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your information is protected with SSL/TLS encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. We never share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
What browsers and devices are supported? +
SafetyCheck Pro™ works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Our platform is fully responsive and works on desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. SMS alerts work on any phone that can receive text messages.

Alerts & Monitoring

How do I receive violation alerts? +
You can receive alerts via:
  • SMS text messages to multiple phone numbers
  • Email notifications to multiple addresses
  • Dashboard notifications when logged in
  • Mobile push notifications (coming soon)
Configure your preferences to receive alerts for specific violation types, severity levels, or drivers.
Can multiple people receive alerts? +
Yes! You can add unlimited users to your account with different roles and alert preferences. Set up alerts for safety managers, dispatchers, drivers, or executives. Each user can customize their notification preferences independently.
What if FMCSA data is delayed? +
While we check FMCSA databases frequently, the initial data entry by inspection officers can sometimes be delayed. Most inspections appear within 24-48 hours, but some may take longer. We alert you as soon as the data becomes available in the FMCSA system.
